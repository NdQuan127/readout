## intro
Have you ever wondered, [[cue:question]]when we talk to an AI, how does the AI actually understand our words?

## token-1
The answer is, it doesn't understand words at all; [[cue:reveal]]it only recognizes tokens.

## token-2
You can think of a token as the AI's smallest unit of information.
For example, the word "artificial intelligence", [[cue:split]]in the AI's eyes, might be split into tokens like "art", "ific", "ial", and so on.

## ending
So the next time you see a phrase like "million-token context window", [[cue:context]]you'll know it refers to how many of these small blocks the AI can remember at one time.
